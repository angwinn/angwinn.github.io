About Me1/20/2019

Hey there! I'm so glad you're here checking out my little blog! I'm trying to make the most of nap times and evenings to cook, bake, wife, and blog. Yes, I just used wife as a verb- mainly because I'm learning to balance being a mom and wife at the same time. My name's Michelle and I'm a 20- something year old Louisiana girl, born and raised in an anything but Southern multi-cultural home. I've been saved by the grace of God, and Lord knows, I rely on that grace daily. I'm married to the most wonderful man and we welcomed our first baby, Maverick, on March 17th of 2016! With all of that being said, I decided to make my dream of being a blogger a reality and created this little gem. Here you will find all of our family's adventures (we're really funny, people- if I do say so myself), first-time mom advice/experiences (because let's be real, this is all a learning process so why not dish out what I'm learning), all things marriage and being first time parents (everyone loves a good 'I just got peed on' story) and so many other things- so go ahead and check out the above menu! I hope this journey of a blog inspires you, makes you laugh, and gives you great ideas.
